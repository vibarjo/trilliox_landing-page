Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How do I get started with NYVRIX? ▼

Simply connect your wallet using MetaMask or your preferred Web3 wallet. Deposit funds, and you're ready to start trading. We have a beginner's guide and video tutorials to help you get started in minutes.

What are the supported networks? ▼

NYVRIX is available on Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism, Avalanche, and Fantom. We're constantly adding support for new networks based on community feedback.

Is my money safe? ▼

Yes. Our smart contracts are audited by CertiK, and we maintain comprehensive insurance through Nexus Mutual. Additionally, your funds are held in non-custodial wallets under your complete control.

What fees do you charge? ▼

We charge 0.25% on swaps and 0.04% on stable swaps. These fees are among the lowest in DeFi and go directly to liquidity providers and the treasury.

How can I earn rewards? ▼

Provide liquidity to earn swap fees. Stake NYVRIX tokens to earn protocol revenue. Participate in governance to earn voting rewards. Multiple ways to grow your wealth.

How do I participate in governance? ▼

Hold NYVRIX tokens and delegate them (to yourself or others). Vote on proposals in our DAO. Top voters earn rewards. Your stake determines your voting power.

Our Services

At Genesis Ledger, we offer a suite of decentralized finance products designed to provide security, transparency, and efficiency for our clients. Below are some core concepts that power our solutions.

What is DeFi?

DeFi, or decentralized finance, uses blockchain technology to eliminate intermediaries in financial transactions.

How DeFi Works

DeFi enables peer-to-peer transactions via smart contracts, such as Uniswap and Aave.

How Blockchain Works

Blockchain is a decentralized, immutable ledger with cryptographically linked blocks.

How Smart Contracts Work

Self-executing programs that automate agreements when certain conditions are met.
